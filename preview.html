About Us

London's Electrical Partner for Demanding Projects

Santana Electrical Services is a London-based electrical contractor specialising in large-scale commercial projects. We work directly with main contractors, developers, and project managers who need a reliable, certified electrical partner from the ground up.

Holding both NICEIC registration and CHAS accreditation — two of the most recognised industry certifications in the UK — we ensure every project meets the highest safety, quality, and compliance standards.
